Find out more about the conditions and the complete route to a driver's license here .

Of course we offer driving lessons with automatic and manual cars. We leave it up to you how you learn to drive. However, the trend is clearly towards automatic transmission: Around 80% of our learner drivers prefer the automatic transmission.

There are mutliple reasons for this:

  • Since February 2019, the driving license no longer states whether the driving test was completed with an automatic or manual transmission.
  • Given today's vehicle market and technological changes, manual transmissions will probably soon be a thing of the past (with a few exceptions). Vehicle manufacturers are increasingly only producing models with automatic transmissions.
  • Technologically and ecologically, automatic transmissions have long overtaken manual ones.

However, since there are still many vehicles with manual transmissions on the market, we recommend our learner drivers to complete a few lessons with the manual transmission during driver training. So you are well prepared for all situations.
